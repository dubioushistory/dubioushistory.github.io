Contact us!

This website is built on a collaborative platform GitHub. If you have any suggestions or contributions, the preferable way to voice them is through the Issues, Pull Requests, or Discussions tabs in the link above. We are always thrilled for any suggestions or opinions!

If you don’t feel like creating an account on GitHub, you can also use plain old e-mail to contact us. Either use the form below or write directly to our address through your mail client.

Also, each article offers a discussion forum provided by Disqus, which allows you to log in via your Google, Facebook, or Twitter account. (To be done.)
