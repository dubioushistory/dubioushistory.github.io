Controversial artifacts

» Kap-Dwa - a two-headed giant

Kap-Dwa is allegedly a mummified body of 12-foot [3.7 m] tall two-headed giant from Patagonia, making him potentially the only material proof of Patagonian giants we have.

» Dighton Rock

The Dighton Rock, crystaline sandstone boulder found in the riverbed of the Taunton River in current Berkley, Massachsetts, bears petroglyphs which are still controversial to this day. This artefact is remarkable mainly for how long it is has been generating range of very diverse speculations.
